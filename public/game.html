How to play

Hangman is a word-guessing game. Click or tap New Game to display the letters on a keyboard and a row of dashes indincating the length of the word to be guessed. Select a letter. If it's in the word, it replaces the dash(es). Each wrong guess results in a part being added to the victim on the gallows. Your goal is to guess the word before the person is completely rendered.
